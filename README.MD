Bot Storage (Powered)

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3753396144

Adds a specialized storage facility where idle bots can be assigned to power down, halting their resource consumption and preventing wear and tear—if you can supply the energy to keep the stasis fields running!

Unlike the standard Bot Storage, this advanced facility requires a connection to your settlement's mechanical power grid to function effectively.

Features:

Dynamic Power Draw: The building scales its power consumption automatically. The more bots you store inside, the more mechanical power the building demands from your network.

Efficiency-Based Preservation: The rate at which your bots age (deteriorate) is directly tied to the building's power efficiency.

100% Power: Bot aging is completely halted.

Partial Power: If the grid is struggling (e.g., 75% efficiency), bots will age slowly (at 25% of their normal speed).

0% Power / Paused: Bots will age at their normal rate, though their resource consumption will remain paused.

Configurable power usage by editing PlayerData\BotStorage.txt

Supported Factions:
IronTeeth, Folktails, Emberpelts, LeafCoats, and Whitepaws.
